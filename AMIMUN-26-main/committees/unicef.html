United Nations International Children's Emergency Fund

About UNICEF

UNICEF advocates for the rights and welfare of children worldwide. The agenda at AMIMUN 2026 will focus on access to education in conflict zones, child refugee crises, and safeguarding children in digital spaces. Delegates will engage with ethical and logistical concerns surrounding child protection, policy frameworks, and sustainable child-centric development models.

UNICEF works to ensure the rights of all children, particularly the most disadvantaged, are realized through advocacy, education, and emergency response programs around the world.

Agenda

Ensuring Universal Access to Education in Conflict-Affected States with Emphasis on the Global South
