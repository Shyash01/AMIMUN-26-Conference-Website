Why AMIMUN

Discover what makes AMIMUN a premier platform for aspiring diplomats and global leaders

About AMIMUN

The Amity International Model United Nations (AMIMUN) Conference serves as a distinguished platform where diplomacy meets intellectual rigor. Through dynamic committees, AMIMUN fosters an environment that enhances critical thinking, public speaking, and leadership skills, empowering participants to address pressing global issues collaboratively. Building on the legacy of its thirteen successful editions, AMIMUN has established itself as a beacon in the Model UN circuit, attracting participants from around the world.

AMIMUN is not just a conference; it is a crucible where future leaders are shaped, equipped to navigate the complexities of an interconnected world.

– Delegate, AMIMUN 2026

Absolute Commitment

The Secretariat of the Amity International Model United Nations Conference is a dedicated team of people who have continuously exhibited their diligence and knowledge. Each individual is precisely chosen to ensure that the principles of AMIMUN are upheld and implemented with the utmost dedication. The secretariat maintains the highest hospitality standard while ensuring every committee's efficient operation. Thereby, we continue to be dedicated to the promise of excellence and assure a fulfilling experience.

International Platform

Amity University is proud to be an institute where individuals from diverse backgrounds interact and work together. AMIMUN, abiding by the same objective, has established an international platform for incorporating ideas, opinions, and perspectives. The participation of international delegates and previous collaborations with organizations such as the United Nations Information Center for India and Bhutan, Amnesty International, and Doctors Without Borders contribute to efficiently imparting knowledge of global affairs, diplomacy, and international policy to students while also bringing authenticity to the experience.

Creativity & Innovation

Amity University works to boost creativity among students. We are advocates of students' overall growth through participation in various disciplines and activities.

Brush Up Ingenuity

AMIMUN has been successful in providing delegates with a platform where they can hone and refine their strategic abilities. The AMIMUN will encourage this manner of thinking to create solutions for some grave world issues.

Engaging & Paramount Motions

The AMIMUN fraternity offers the most intriguing motions to encourage the highest quality of debate. Deliberation on challenging global issues will sharpen the members with the learnings of the United Nations and assist them with understanding the current world request.

About Amity University

Our Vision: To strengthen society as a whole by delivering communal, collaborative instruction, leading the world in skill development, and living up to our commitment to being the institution where modernity and tradition coexist. Our mission is to provide education, address all learning levels in the constantly changing field of contemporary events, expand the frontiers of learning and research, and examine and enhance students' overall character. We not only create great experts but well-rounded individuals. Our students understand and respect human qualities, have pride in their heritage and culture, discern what is right, and imbibe attributes of courage, conviction, and action.

Our Core Values

Academic Excellence

Amity University aspires to the highest quality and excellence in teaching, learning, research, and scholarship across all disciplines.

Integrity & Ethics

Amity University is committed to academic liberty, transparency, and accountability, as well as the highest ethical values, transparency, and professionalism.

Diversity & Mutual Respect

Amity University fosters a culture of safety, trust, and mutual respect and incorporates equality and diversity into its strategy by ensuring that strategic plans are fair and inclusive.

Expanding Horizons of Knowledge

Amity University is inspired by research and innovation and offers broad participation in scholarly activities when pursuing change, creativity, and quality.

Shared Governance

Amity University promotes shared decision-making through a process based on collaborative consultation, open information flow, diversified participation, and collective deliberation of all stakeholders.

Social Responsibility

Amity University promotes and maintains an inclusive atmosphere in which everyone can achieve their full potential and contribute to the betterment of society.

Environmental Responsibility

Amity University is entirely aware of its environmental responsibilities and strives for the principle of sustainable development to ensure any adverse environmental impact of its activities.

Service

Amity University strives to address its constituents' diverse personal and professional development needs while also encouraging the habit of involvement, caring, and civic responsibility by emphasizing the connection among service, excellence, and professional growth.
